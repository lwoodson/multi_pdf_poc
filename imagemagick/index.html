Option 3 - imagemagick

Convert the PDF to JPG and render inline in a printer friendly view of the auth-group specific application form.

  • No new dependencies
  • Least issues with formatting
  • JPG file size is large (1.4 meg vs 262k).
  • JPG does not compress well (only to 1.1 meg).
  • Time to generate the JPG is slow (~ 30 secs)
  • Can probably tune settings for some time improvement
  • Fidelity will be affected
